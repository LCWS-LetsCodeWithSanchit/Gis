Frequently Asked Questions

Detailed insights into our technical GIS workflows and project delivery.

What specific data formats can you process? ▼
We work with a wide range of industry-standard formats:
  • Vector: Shapefiles, GeoJSON, KML, GDB.
  • Raster: GeoTIFF, Cloud Optimized GeoTIFF (COG), JPEG2000.
  • Advanced: LiDAR (LAS/LAZ), Point Clouds, and CAD drawings.
How long does a typical GIS project take? ▼

Timelines vary by complexity. Small spatial analysis or static map production typically takes 3-5 business days. Complex Web-GIS application development or large-scale data automation pipelines can take 3-6 weeks depending on integration requirements.

Can you integrate maps into our existing company dashboard? ▼

Yes. We specialize in building embeddable components using Leaflet.js, Mapbox GL JS, or ArcGIS API. We can build custom React or Vue components that pull data directly from your internal APIs to show real-time spatial updates on your current platform.

Do you provide satellite imagery or do I need to supply it? ▼

We can assist with both. We utilize free open-source imagery (Sentinel-2, Landsat 8/9) for many environmental projects. For high-resolution commercial needs (Maxar, Planet), we facilitate the procurement and handle all atmospheric correction and band math processing.
